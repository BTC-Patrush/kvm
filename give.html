Generosity

Giving is an Act of Worship.

Your generosity enables us to minister to our city and make a difference in the world. Thank you for partnering with us in this mission.

Before You Give

We seek to follow the example and teaching of our Lord Jesus in financial matters. When people ask us whether they can support this ministry, we ask them to go through this checklist first.

1

Are you a born-again child of God?

Do you agree with our doctrinal statement? It is a great honour and privilege to support the work of God on earth; but that privilege is given only to His born-again children. (3 John 7)

What We Believe
2

Do you have enough for your family?

Are you sure that your giving will not put your family under financial strain? You must take care of your family's needs first. Our Father in heaven does not want any of His children to suffer just because they gave Him money. (Mark 7:9-13, 1 Tim 5:8)

3

Do you have large debts to repay?

If so, please clear those debts first. God wants His children to live restful lives, free from debt. We must give to Caesar what is his before giving to God. (Matt 22:21, Rom 13:8)

Note: A house-loan is not considered a "debt" in this context as it is an asset of equal value. Similarly for insured vehicle loans.
4

Do you have a clear conscience?

Have you done your best to reconcile with those you have hurt in any way? God will not accept an offering from anyone who has hurt someone and still not apologised. (Matt 5:23-24)

5

Are you giving freely and cheerfully?

God loves cheerful givers, not reluctant ones. He does not want gifts given under pressure, obligation, or merely to ease a conscience. (2 Cor 9:7)

Where does it go?

We believe in financial transparency and stewardship. Every rupee given to KVM is used to further the kingdom of God in Kathmandu and beyond.

Local Outreach

Feeding programs, community cleanups, and support for local families in need.

Next Generation

Investing in our kids and youth ministries, camps, and discipleship materials.

Ministry Operations

Keeping the lights on, staff support, and maintaining our facility for weekly gatherings.

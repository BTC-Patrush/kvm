Who We Are

Our Story & Vision

A community of believers in the heart of Kathmandu, dedicated to seeing lives transformed by the love of Christ.

Our History

From a Small Gathering to a Movement.

Kathmandu Vijaya Mandali (KVM) began in 2010 with just a handful of believers gathering in a small living room, united by a hunger for God's presence and a burden for the city of Kathmandu.

Over the last decade, God has faithfully grown our family. What started as a small prayer meeting has blossomed into a vibrant community serving hundreds of families across the valley. Through every season, our heartbeat has remained the same: to see Jesus lifted high in Nepal.

Est. 2010

Kathmandu, Nepal

Our Mission

To proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ, disciple believers into maturity, and serve the needy in our community with compassion, grace, and the tangible love of God.

Our Vision

To see Kathmandu transformed by God's love, raising a generation of Spirit-empowered leaders who walk in victory (Vijaya) and influence every sphere of society with truth.

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, authoritative Word of God, our perfect guide for faith and life.

Salvation

We believe salvation is a free gift of grace from God, received through faith in Jesus Christ alone.

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.

Holy Spirit

We believe in the active presence of the Holy Spirit, empowering believers for witness and service.

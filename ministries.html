Our Ministries

There is a place for you here. Find your community and use your gifts to serve.

We are one body with many parts.

At Kathmandu Vijaya Mandali, we believe that church isn't just a building you walk into, but a family you belong to. Our ministries are designed to help you connect with others in your stage of life and grow deeper in your relationship with Christ.

Ages 0 - 12

Vijaya Kids

We create a safe, fun, and engaging environment where children can learn about Jesus on their level. Every Sunday, our dedicated team leads kids through worship, Bible stories, and interactive games.

KVM Youth

Helping the next generation navigate life with faith. Our youth ministry is a place for teenagers to belong, ask hard questions, and discover their purpose in God. We meet weekly for high-energy worship and relevant teaching.

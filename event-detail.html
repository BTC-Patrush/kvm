Featured Event

Christmas Eve Service

Date December 24, 2025
Time 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM

About the Event

Join us for a magical evening of carols, candlelight, and the story of Jesus' birth. Bring your whole family for this special one-hour service designed to focus our hearts on the true meaning of the season.

We will have special performances by our kids' choir, a brief message of hope from Pastor John, and we will end the night by lighting candles and singing "Silent Night" together. Hot cocoa and cookies will be served in the lobby immediately following the service.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there childcare available?
Yes! We have childcare available for ages 0-3 in our Nursery. Children ages 4 and up are invited to sit with their families in the main service. We have activity packs available for them in the lobby.
Where should I park?
We have a main parking lot directly in front of the building. If that fills up, our parking team will direct you to the overflow lot across the street.
What should I wear?
Come as you are! Some people like to dress up for Christmas Eve, while others prefer jeans and sweaters. You will fit in either way.
